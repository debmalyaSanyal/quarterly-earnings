Key Financial Formula (ROIC)

We monitor Return on Invested Capital:

$$ \mathrm{ROIC} = \frac{\text{NOPAT}}{\text{Invested Capital}} = \frac{\text{EBIT}\times(1-t)}{\text{Debt}+\text{Equity}} $$

  • NOPAT = Net Operating Profit After Tax
  • Invested Capital = Debt + Equity
  • t = effective tax rate

Python: CAGR calculation


def cagr(start, end, years):
    """Compound Annual Growth Rate"""
    return (end/start)**(1/years) - 1

print(f"CAGR: {cagr(1000,1400,3):.2%}")
