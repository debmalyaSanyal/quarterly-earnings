Key Financial Formula

We monitor Return on Invested Capital (ROIC):

$$ \mathrm{ROIC} = \frac{\text{NOPAT}}{\text{Invested\ Capital}} = \frac{\text{EBIT} \times (1 - t)}{ \text{Debt} + \text{Equity} } $$

  • NOPAT = Net Operating Profit After Tax
  • Invested Capital = Debt + Equity
  • t = effective tax rate
Explain the formula and why ROIC is meaningful. Show how small improvements in EBIT amplify ROIC.

Sample: Calculating CAGR (Python)


def cagr(start_value, end_value, years):
    """
    Compound Annual Growth Rate.
    """
    return (end_value / start_value) ** (1.0 / years) - 1

# Example
print(f"CAGR: {cagr(1000, 1280, 3):.2%}")
