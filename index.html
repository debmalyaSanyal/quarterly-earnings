Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)

We rely on ROIC to evaluate financial efficiency:

$$ \text{ROIC} = \frac{\text{EBIT} \cdot (1 - t)} {\text{Debt} + \text{Equity}} $$

  • EBIT = Earnings Before Interest & Tax
  • t = Effective tax rate
  • Capital = Debt + Equity
Explain what each variable means, then discuss ROIC trends.

Python Example: CAGR


def cagr(start, end, years):
    """
    Calculate Compound Annual Growth Rate.
    """
    return (end / start) ** (1/years) - 1

print(cagr(1000, 1400, 3))
